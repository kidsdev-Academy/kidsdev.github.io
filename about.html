From Sunderland to the World

KidsDev began right here in Sunderland with a simple idea: coding shouldn't be boring, and it shouldn't be a luxury.

We realized that while technology is everywhere, true digital literacy is rare. We wanted to create a space where kids could move from being consumers to creators.

Today, we have evolved into a Global Tech Group. We offer free resources, daily challenges, and expert mentorship to help students master Python, Web Development, and Game Design.

Core Values

What makes our group different?

Accessibility First

Education shouldn't have a paywall. Our core learning materials are open to everyone, everywhere.

Creativity Over Syntax

We focus on building actual projects. Logic comes first; memorizing complex code comes second.

Safe Environment

A supportive, bully-free zone where making mistakes is celebrated as part of the learning process.
