Our Mission

Empowering the
Next Generation

We believe every child deserves the chance to understand the technology shaping their world. We don't just teach code; we teach creativity, logic, and problem-solving.

How We Started

KidsDev began right here in Sunderland with a simple idea: coding shouldn't be boring, and it shouldn't be expensive.

We realized that while technology is everywhere, true digital literacy is rare. We wanted to create a space where kids could move from being consumers to creators.

Today, we offer free resources, daily challenges, and structured courses to help students take their first steps into Python, Web Development, and Game Design.

Core Values

What makes KidsDev different?

Accessibility First

Education shouldn't have a paywall. That's why our core resources are free for everyone, everywhere.

Creativity Over Syntax

We focus on building actual projects. Logic comes first; memorizing complex code comes second.

Safe Environment

A supportive, bully-free zone where making mistakes is celebrated as part of the learning process.
