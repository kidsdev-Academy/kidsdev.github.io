Design a Superhero Card with CSS

By Tolani Agbeyangi

October 25, 2025 â€¢ 8 min read

HTML is like the skeleton of a website, but CSS is the costume! Today, we will create a digital ID card for your favorite superhero.

Step 1: The Skeleton (HTML)

We need a container (a div) to hold our hero's name and power.

index.html
<div class="card">
  <h2>Super Coder</h2>
  <p>Power: Infinite Logic</p>
</div>

Step 2: The Costume (CSS)

Now, let's make it look epic. We will use `background-color`, `border-radius` (for rounded corners), and `box-shadow` to give it a comic book style.

style.css
.card {
  background-color: orange;
  padding: 20px;
  border-radius: 15px;
  box-shadow: 8px 8px 0px #0F172A;
  color: white;
}
