How the Internet Works

The Big Wire

The internet feels invisible, like magic in the air. But actually, it is physical! The internet is basically a massive wire (often buried deep underwater) that connects your computer to giant computers called Servers.

When you visit a website like YouTube, your computer sends a request through these wires to a server in a Google data center. The server finds the video you want and sends it back to you.

Clients vs Servers

Client (You)

Laptops, Phones, iPads. Devices that ask for information.

Server (The Web)

Powerful computers that store websites and never turn off.
