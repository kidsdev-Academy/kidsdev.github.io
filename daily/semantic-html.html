Stop Using <div> for Everything

A <div> tells the browser "This is a box". It doesn't say if it's a header, a footer, or an article. This is bad for Google (SEO) and bad for blind users (Screen Readers).

Use Semantic Tags instead. They give meaning to your code.

Bad Practice

<div id="header">
<div id="nav">
<div id="footer">

Good Practice

<header>
<nav>
<footer>
