VS Code Magic Shortcuts

1. The Multi-Cursor

Type in multiple places at once!

Alt + Click

2. Move Line Up/Down

Don't cut and paste. Just move the whole line.

Alt + ⬆ / ⬇

3. Find Anything

Open any file instantly without looking at the sidebar.

Ctrl + P
