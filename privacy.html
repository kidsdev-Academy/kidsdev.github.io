Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

At KidsDev Academy, we prioritize the privacy and safety of our students above all else. This policy outlines exactly what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our educational services, we collect the following limited information:

  • Account Information: Name, Email Address, and encrypted Password (for login purposes).
  • Progress Data: Course completion status, XP points, and badges earned to track learning.
  • Technical Data: Browser type and device type to ensure our website works correctly on your screen.

2. How We Use Data

We do not sell your data. We use your information solely to:

  • Create and manage your personal dashboard.
  • Save your progress so you can resume courses where you left off.
  • Send important updates regarding the platform (e.g., password resets).

3. Children's Safety & COPPA

Since our audience includes children, we take extra precautions:

  • No Direct Messaging: Students cannot send private messages to each other.
  • No Personal Ads: We do not display third-party advertisements based on user behavior.
  • Profile Privacy: User profiles are not public and cannot be indexed by search engines.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to run our platform:

  • Google Firebase: For secure authentication and database hosting.
  • Netlify: For hosting our website files.
