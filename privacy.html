Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Safety First

At KidsDev, the safety and privacy of children is our top priority. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13 without parental consent.

2. Information We Collect

Currently, this website is a showcase portfolio and educational resource. We do not require account creation or login.

  • Contact Forms: If you use our contact form, we receive the name and email you provide solely to reply to your inquiry.
  • Analytics: We may use anonymous tools to see how many people visit our site, but we do not track individual users.

3. External Links

Our site contains links to external websites (like GitHub or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage parents to monitor their children's online activity.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@kidsdev.com.
