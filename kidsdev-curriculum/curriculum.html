Our Methodology

1

Understand

We start with a simple question: "What problem are we solving?" Understanding the goal makes coding easier.

2

Build Together

Students type along with the instructor. We make mistakes together, fix bugs together, and learn how things work.

3

At the end of the term, every student presents their own "Capstone Project" to the class to build confidence.

Common Questions

Do I need previous experience?

Not at all! Level 0 is perfect for complete beginners. We guide you step-by-step.

Can I use an iPad/Tablet?

For Level 1 (Scratch), a tablet is okay. But for Level 2 and up, you MUST have a laptop (Windows or Mac) because real coding requires typing on a keyboard.

What happens if I miss a class?

We record all online classes so you can watch them later. For physical classes, instructors will help you catch up before the next lesson starts.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes! You can pay 60% when you start and the remaining 40% by the 5th week of class.
